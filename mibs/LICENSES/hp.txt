Hewlett-Packard Company
=======================

Tag applied to MIB files whose first 200 lines contain a copyright
notice attributable to Hewlett-Packard (the pre-2015-split entity)
or its consumer-and-printer successor HP Inc.

The notice typically reads:

  "Copyright (c) <year> Hewlett-Packard Company. All rights reserved."

followed by terms governing redistribution. The post-2015 enterprise
network products (ProCurve, ProLiant) live under the HPE tag — see
`hpe.txt`.

For the authoritative license terms, see the MIB's own header
comment block.
