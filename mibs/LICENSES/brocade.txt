Brocade Communications
======================

Tag applied to MIB files whose first 200 lines contain a copyright
notice attributable to Brocade Communications Systems (now split
across Broadcom for storage networking and Extreme Networks for
the Ruckus / IP-networking line).

The notice typically reads:

  "Copyright (c) <year> Brocade Communications Systems, Inc."

followed by terms governing redistribution. For the authoritative
license terms, see the MIB's own header comment block.
