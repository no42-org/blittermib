Juniper Networks
================

Tag applied to MIB files whose first 200 lines contain a copyright
notice attributable to Juniper Networks, Inc.

The notice typically reads:

  "Copyright (c) <year> Juniper Networks, Inc. All rights reserved."

followed by terms governing redistribution. Juniper publishes its
MIB collection at:

  https://www.juniper.net/documentation/mib

For the authoritative license terms, see the MIB's own header
comment block.
