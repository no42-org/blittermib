RFC Editor / IETF Trust
=======================

Tag applied to MIB files whose first 200 lines contain a copyright
notice attributable to The Internet Society or IETF Trust. These
MIBs are published as part of an RFC (or Internet-Draft) and are
governed by the IETF Trust's Trust Legal Provisions ("TLP").

The TLP allows verbatim distribution and re-publication subject to
attribution requirements. The current revision is at:

  https://trustee.ietf.org/license-info/

Each MIB's verbatim copyright notice in its own header is the
authoritative source. The notice typically reads:

  "Copyright (c) <year> IETF Trust and the persons identified as
   authors of the code. All rights reserved."

followed by terms permitting redistribution.
