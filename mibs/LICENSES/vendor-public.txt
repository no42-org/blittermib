Vendor Public (catch-all)
=========================

Tag applied via `_overrides.yaml` to MIB files where:

  1. The vendor's copyright notice doesn't match any of the more
     specific starter-set tags (cisco, juniper, hpe, hp, aruba,
     huawei, a10, mellanox, brocade, extreme), AND
  2. The vendor's published MIB carries terms permitting public
     redistribution.

This is a maintainer-curated tag; the auto-detector NEVER produces
`vendor-public`. Use it sparingly — prefer adding a vendor-specific
pattern to `cmd/mib-index/license.go::licensePatterns` when a new
vendor accumulates several MIBs in the corpus.

For the authoritative license terms of any individual MIB, see
the MIB's own header comment block.
