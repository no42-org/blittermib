Huawei Technologies
===================

Tag applied to MIB files whose first 200 lines contain a copyright
notice attributable to Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd.

The notice typically reads:

  "Copyright (c) <year> Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd. All rights
   reserved."

followed by terms governing redistribution. Huawei publishes MIBs
through its support portal at:

  https://support.huawei.com/

For the authoritative license terms, see the MIB's own header
comment block.
