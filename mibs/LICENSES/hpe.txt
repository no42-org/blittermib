Hewlett Packard Enterprise
==========================

Tag applied to MIB files whose first 200 lines contain a copyright
notice attributable to Hewlett Packard Enterprise (the post-2015
enterprise spinoff covering networking, server, and storage
products).

The notice typically reads:

  "Copyright (c) <year> Hewlett Packard Enterprise Development LP."

followed by terms governing redistribution. Pre-2015 HP networking
MIBs and the HP Inc. (consumer + printer) line use the `hp` tag —
see `hp.txt`.

For the authoritative license terms, see the MIB's own header
comment block.
