Cisco Systems
=============

Tag applied to MIB files whose first 200 lines contain a copyright
notice attributable to Cisco Systems, Inc.

The license text varies by MIB but typically appears as:

  "Copyright (c) <year> Cisco Systems, Inc. All rights reserved."

followed by terms governing redistribution within Cisco's published
SNMP framework. Cisco publishes its MIB collection at:

  https://www.cisco.com/c/en/us/support/web/tools/snmp/mibs.html

For the authoritative license terms, see the MIB's own header
comment block.
