Extreme Networks
================

Tag applied to MIB files whose first 200 lines contain a copyright
notice attributable to Extreme Networks, Inc.

The notice typically reads:

  "Copyright (c) <year> Extreme Networks, Inc. All rights reserved."

followed by terms governing redistribution. MIBs originating from
acquired companies (Enterasys, Aerohive, Brocade IP networking)
that retain the original vendor's copyright notice can be tagged
`vendor-public` via `_overrides.yaml` when the lineage is clear,
or left at `unknown` for maintainer review.

For the authoritative license terms, see the MIB's own header
comment block.
