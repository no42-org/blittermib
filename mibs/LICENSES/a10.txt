A10 Networks
============

Tag applied to MIB files whose first 200 lines contain a copyright
notice attributable to A10 Networks, Inc.

The notice typically reads:

  "Copyright (c) <year> A10 Networks. All rights reserved."

followed by terms governing redistribution. A10's MIB collection
ships with the ACOS / Thunder product lines.

For the authoritative license terms, see the MIB's own header
comment block.
