Aruba Networks
==============

Tag applied to MIB files whose first 200 lines contain a copyright
notice attributable to Aruba Networks (now an HPE subsidiary).

The notice typically reads:

  "Copyright (c) <year> Aruba Networks, Inc. All rights reserved."

followed by terms governing redistribution. Aruba's MIB collection
lives at:

  https://asp.arubanetworks.com/

For the authoritative license terms, see the MIB's own header
comment block.
