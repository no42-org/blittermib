What this means

When smilint reports a missing identifier, it usually means one of three things: the referenced module wasn't supplied, the IMPORTS clause is incomplete, or the symbol name is misspelled. Vendor MIBs sometimes ship with implicit dependencies that net-snmp accepts and stricter parsers reject.

Lenient mode downgrades these errors to warnings and parses what it can. The resulting module pages will be marked as partial — symbols that depend on unresolved references will be stubbed.
